# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It helps companies manage:
- customer information,
- sales,
- communication,
- support,
- business workflow.

CRM improves customer relationships and helps businesses stay organized.

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because:
- it stores all customer data in one place,
- improves sales tracking,
- automates business processes,
- improves customer support,
- helps teams work efficiently.

Salesforce is cloud-based, secure, and scalable.

---

## What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used by companies to manage customers, sales, and business operations.

It helps businesses:
- track leads,
- manage customers,
- automate workflows,
- improve productivity.

---

## Salesforce Objects

### Account
Represents a company or organization.

Example:
- Amazon
- Infosys
- Hospital
- College

---

### Contact
Represents a person related to an account.

Example:
- Student
- Employee
- Patient

---

### Opportunity
Represents a potential business deal or sales opportunity.

Example:
- Admission process
- Treatment package
- Product purchase

---

### Lead
Represents a potential customer or enquiry.

Example:
- Student asking for admission information
- Customer asking about a product

---

## Business Workflow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

### Example (College Admission)

1. Student asks about admission → Lead
2. Student submits details → Contact
3. Admission process starts → Opportunity
4. Student joins college → Customer

---
