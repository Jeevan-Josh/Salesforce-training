# Personal Notes - Day 1

## Important Concepts

### CRM
CRM helps businesses manage customer relationships and business workflows.

### Salesforce
Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform.

### Account
Represents a company or organization.

### Contact
Represents a person.

### Opportunity
Represents a possible business deal.

### Lead
Represents a potential customer.
